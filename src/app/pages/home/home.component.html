What we do

At Minack Flooring & Co, we specialize in providing premium flooring solutions tailored to meet the unique needs of our clients. With a wide range of high-quality options, including carpet, timber, European Oak, laminate, hybrid flooring, parquetry, chevron, vinyl plank, and carpet tiles, we have something for every style and space.

Our services go beyond just offering flooring products. We provide expert advice to help you choose the perfect flooring for your home or business. From concept to installation, our skilled team ensures a seamless experience, delivering exceptional craftsmanship and attention to detail.

We also offer custom designs for intricate flooring styles such as parquetry and chevron, allowing you to create a space that is truly one-of-a-kind. Whether you're looking for durability, elegance, or functionality, our eco-friendly and sustainable options cater to both residential and commercial needs.
